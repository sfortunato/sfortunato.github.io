Reading / Watching List

People from a variety of backgrounds (engineers, designers, high schoolers...) ask me what they should read to learn more about product management. I think product and startups are fundamentally intertwined (but maybe that's just because I like starting products) so this is a bit of a mix of both.

4 Steps to the Epiphany

The original source of customer development (aka product / market fit).

Escaping The Build Trap

Building products doesn't mean they're good or useful. Build less to build better.

Lean Startup

Accessible, standard book that is surprisingly cheap.

Paul Graham Blog

Don't agree with all of these anymore but definitely worth reading.

The Agile Manifesto

Oh my god it's so short just read it again and again.

Y Combinator Videos

The 'Startup School' ones are particularly good.

Product Hunt

If you want to creep on product people talking about products.

Quora

Lots of different content, but some great threads about building products.

Treehouse - Learn to Code!

Best learn-to-code platform I've encountered (by far). Do it!
